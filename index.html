Philippines

The Philippines is an archipelagic country located in Southeast Asia, consisting of over 7,000 islands in the western Pacific Ocean. It is bounded by the South China Sea to the west and the Pacific Ocean to the east. The capital city is Manila, and the largest island is Luzon, which includes the capital and most populous city, Quezon City. The country has a rich cultural heritage, influenced by Spanish and American colonial periods, and is known for its diverse languages, primarily Pilipino and English. The Philippines is one of the most populous countries in Asia, with a population exceeding 112 million.
